Features

Everything you need to take control of your browsing.

Instant Browser Picker

A compact popup appears right when you click a link. See all your browsers at a glance with their icons and keyboard shortcuts.

Browser Profiles

See your Chrome and Firefox profiles alongside your browsers. Open links directly in your Work, Personal, or any other profile.

Keyboard First

Press 1–9 to instantly open a link in a specific browser. Esc to dismiss. No mouse needed.

Full Browser Management

Rearrange browsers with drag & drop, give them nicknames, and customize how they appear in the picker. Your setup, your way.

Automation Rules Pro

Create rules that automatically route URLs to the right browser. Match by domain, regex pattern, or even by which app opened the link.

Smart Suggestions Pro

Turnstile learns from your choices and suggests automation rules when it detects patterns. Say yes, and it handles everything automatically.

How it works

Three steps to browser freedom.

1

Click any link

In Slack, Teams, email, or wherever. Turnstile intercepts it before it reaches a browser.

2

Pick a browser

A compact picker appears with your browsers and profiles. Press a number key or click.

3

Done

The link opens in your chosen browser. Set up rules, and Turnstile will do it automatically next time.
